## Post 3 - “Best Category” Listicles and AI Overviews

**Date:** 2026-06-23  
**Source:** LinkedIn activity feed  
**Post link:** Not collected

**Summary:**  
Lily explains that publishing "best [category]" listicles used to be an easy way to influence AI answers, especially in B2B categories. However, her research suggests that being cited as a source and actually being recommended in AI Overviews are different outcomes.

**Why it matters for AI-powered SEO content production:**  
For B2B SaaS, creating AI-targeted listicles is not enough. Content teams need to understand whether their pages are helping the brand get recommended or simply helping AI systems cite a page that may benefit competitors.

**Key takeaway:**  
AI SEO success should measure recommendations, not only citations.
